About

Eatponies is a website for fans of Multiuser Sketchpad : a website where you could waste your time drawing with other people. Multiuser Sketchpad is now closed so you can draw here. But, instead of drawing on a blank page, you can draw over any webpage. It's something between Goggles and Multiuser Sketchpad.

How it works:

Bookmark the next link (drag it to your favorites) then go to any (non-https) website and click on the bookmark. Then you will be able to draw with people on the same webpage.
This is the link to bookmark.

Limitations

  • Works only in Firefox, Chrome and Epiphany.
  • You only have a 2000x3000 px surface to draw on.
  • URLs are limited to 251 characters.
  • Only 20 people can draw on the same webpage simultaneously.
  • The eraser lags.
